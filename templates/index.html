Frequently Asked Questions

Is UrlForge a Plugin, a SaaS, or a Desktop App? +

UrlForge is an Autonomous Backend Engine. While it has a Web UI, it functions primarily as a "DevSecOps Plugin" for your website repository. It plugs into your codebase, analyzes it, and pushes fixes directly to your GitHub/Vercel.

Can I deploy this on Render.com? +

Yes! UrlForge is fully containerized and production-ready for Render.com. We provide a render.yaml blueprint. Once deployed on Render, it runs as your centralized SEO brain. You can log into your Render-hosted UrlForge dashboard and tell it to fix any of your remote websites.

Do I need to pay for API credits? +

It's up to you. You can use your own OpenAI or Google Gemini API keys. However, if you want a 100% free and private experience, you can point UrlForge to a local Ollama instance and run open-source models like Llama 3 entirely for free.

Will it break my website's design? +

No. The html_rewriter engine strictly manipulates only specific SEO tags (like <title>, <meta>, <h1>) and safely injects headers without touching your structural CSS classes or complex JS frameworks. Plus, you get to review all changes before they are deployed.
